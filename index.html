Why skateboarding?

Skateboarding is something that is just fun and moreover improves your agility, strength and even visual thinking

Ollie

Ollie is the basic trick in skateboarding. Might not be the simplest though, but it all starts from here

  1. Push the tail of your deck as much as you can and jump at the same time.
  2. After jumping start moving the other foot towards deck's nose
  3. If done right - you've done an ollie!

Kickflip

Kickflip falls into the category of basic tricks but it's a bit more complicated because we add a flip to the ollie

  1. Push the tail of your deck as much as you can and jump at the same time.
  2. After jumping start moving the other foot towards deck's nose, but not the middle part, more to the side (where your back looks)
  3. If done right - it will flip and you'll land safely!

360 Flip

360 Flip is an advanced trick, it combines 2 of simple tricks: Kickflip and Shuvit.

  1. Push the tail of your deck as much as you can sliding it inwards and jump at the same time.
  2. After jumping start moving the other foot towards deck's nose (like a Kickflip)
  3. If done right - it will flip, spin and you'll land safely!
